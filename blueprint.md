# Blueprint

## Overview

This document outlines the plan for developing the Flutter application.

## Current Plan

### Splash Screen

*   Create a splash screen that displays for 10 seconds.
*   The splash screen will have a white background.
*   A logo will be displayed in the center with a fade-in animation.
*   A custom loading animation with three bouncing dots will be displayed at the bottom.
*   After 10 seconds, the app will navigate to the home page.
